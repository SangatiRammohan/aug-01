 The story of "Leo" revolves around a mild-mannered man named Parthiban, who leads a peaceful life with his family in a hill station. However, his life takes a dark turn when his past, filled with violence and bloodshed, comes back to haunt him. As Parthiban is forced to confront his buried secrets, the film delves into his transformation into "Leo," a feared and notorious figure with a mysterious and dangerous past.

        The narrative is marked by intense action sequences, psychological tension, and a gripping storyline that keeps the audience on the edge of their seats. The film also explores themes of redemption, revenge, and the duality of human nature.
